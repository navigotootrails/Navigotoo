## The Experience

Mount Batur is Bali's most accessible volcanic trek and arguably its most rewarding. Beginning at 2am from the village of Toya Bungkah, you'll wind through volcanic rock and lava fields in the dark, guided by torchlight, until you reach the 1,717m summit just in time for sunrise.

## The Summit

From the top, the view stretches over Lake Batur far below, Mount Agung to the east, and — on clear mornings — the islands of Lombok and Nusa Penida on the horizon. Your guide will cook a simple breakfast of banana and egg on the volcanic steam vents at the top.

## Practical Details

- Departs: 2:00am from Toya Bungkah
- Returns: ~10:00am
- Trek duration: ~2 hours ascent, ~1.5 hours descent
- Difficulty: Moderate (some loose volcanic scree)

## What's Included

- Certified local guide
- Torches
- Breakfast at the summit (banana, boiled egg, hot drinks)
- Transport from Ubud or Kuta available on request

This experience is best combined with a few days in Ubud to acclimatise and explore Bali's cultural heartland.
